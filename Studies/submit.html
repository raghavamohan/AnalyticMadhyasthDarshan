Study format Contributing guide
House-style checklist (topical studies)
  • Opening intro, then a ## Standpoint and scope section (four short paragraphs).
  • Continuous scholarly prose — avoid scaffold labels like **Step N—**, **Verdict:**, or ## How to read this study.
  • ### N.M Descriptive title subsections for argument steps; cross-reference with §2.3.
  • A ## References section grouping sources, linking ../References/… or external URLs.
  • Author name and slug stay consistent with the approved proposal.
